<p>A neutral or walking pose is perfectly fine for me. I think pose is not that important on skeletals, as their goal is to show a realistic anatomical depiction of an animal's skeleton. So I don't think they need to be too 'spectacular'. And anyway, your skeletals are ALWAYS spectacular, no matter the pose you use. <br>"Live" reconstructions are on a completely different league, though...</p>
