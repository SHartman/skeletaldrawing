<p>The skull is fairly complete, though the lacrimal and jugal are missing, so the exact angle of snout to back of the skull isn't known. There aren't any cervicals either, but the back and tail are well constrained. There's some cross-scaling necessary from other specimens, but nothing too bad - I wouldn't expect any radical changes at this point, but hey, we'll see what more data brings!</p>
