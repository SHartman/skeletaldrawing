<p>There are no hip or leg bones in one specimen (Stromer's). The new specimen lets us restore the pelvis and legs, but to since the two specimens are different sizes you have to cross-scale using the vertebrae to do so. It appears that the new paper made an error in that scaling process, so I've presented a corrected version above based on their own measurements.</p>
