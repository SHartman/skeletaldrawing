<p>Honestly, since genera aren't real natural groupings it probably doesn't matter much. I've changed a lot on this subject, and now prefer monospecific genera for at least Mesozoic and earlier vertebrates, in part to make sure we don't accidentally hide diversity by adding one specimen in a multi-species genus to a phylogenetic analysis.</p>
