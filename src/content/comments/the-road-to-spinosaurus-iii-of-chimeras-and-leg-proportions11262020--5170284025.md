<p>My understanding is that Markgraf was known to Stromer for some time and was a hired field hand on expeditions with him, which has different incentives than finding local fossils to sell piece by piece. But I haven't read an in-depth biography so I may be mistaken. Regardless, I do worry that Spino B could be a chimera. But it has less extraordinary proportions and was geographically closer to the type, so I still prefer it as a model for Spinosaurus.</p>
