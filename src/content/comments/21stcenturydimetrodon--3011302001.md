<p>It's Dimetrodon angelensis, the last species of the genus, known by an incomplete skull with lower jaw and some vertebrae. The taxonomy of Dimetrodon requires a revision, maybe this giant species belongs to a distinct genus. It was a contemporary of the giant Cotylorhynchus hancocki.</p>
