Both the Supersaurus and Apatosaurus legs in the outlined skeletals shown here are so lacking in musculature as to be almost impossible. It also seems to me that taking this point of view of fleshing out the animals skews the data badly on their mass AND their proportions. HOW can a Tyrannosaurus leg when alive be thinner than the bones? Is an assumption being made that the bones swelled in diameter after death? Where is the cartilage?
