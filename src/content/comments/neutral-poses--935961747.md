<p>My favourite pose so far is the walk. I don't think many people would have a problem biomechanically with legged animals walking. It's similar to the typical GSP running pose which makes comparison to other skeletals easier. <br><br>As for neck and tail posture I personally prefer neutral posture, (or close to it). GSP for example poses sauropods with their neck raised to about the same degree. I think that if you're trying to understand sauropod necks it's useful to see differences in their basic neck posture.</p>
