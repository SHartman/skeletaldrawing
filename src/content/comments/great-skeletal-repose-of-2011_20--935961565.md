<p>Paul - You're correct that it's not every single bone, I only isolate single bones that are likely to move (e.g. the limbs), while say the dorsal vertebrae are kept as a functional unit (with the ribs on a separate layer on top of them).<br><br>As disk space and RAM have gotten cheap and plentiful I've been able to work at a high enough resolution that degradation is generally not a serious issue at print resolutions (let alone web use), but in the past I did indeed run into that problem.<br><br>I did also consider Illustrator for that reason, but in the end I decided against it as for me at least, I find I am less precise in my lines when using vector tools, and I want to create a literal representation of the bone outlines as much as possible.</p>
