<p>Happily, I wrote about BROODING feathers both in the scientific literature (Feathered Dragons, 2004) and in fiction (Dinosaur Wars, 2000). Perhaps now this juvenile/adult dichotomy will provoke people to have another look at what I was saying: the whole theropod-to-bird transition was driven by brooding, not flight.</p>
