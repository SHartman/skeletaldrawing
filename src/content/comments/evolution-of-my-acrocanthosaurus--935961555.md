<p>I'd get rid of cartilaginous sterna and sternal ribs in general, since nothing else cartilaginous is ever included in these recons.  But I think we had that discussion before...<br><br>This is a good place to spread the fact that NCSM 14345 does not include manual phalanges II-2 or III-3, manual ungual III, or the tips of manual unguals I and II. These were illustrated and described by Currie and Carpenter (2000), but that was just based on casts made by BHI personnel. I personally find it sad/hilarious that casts managed to be described and illustrated as real bones.</p>
