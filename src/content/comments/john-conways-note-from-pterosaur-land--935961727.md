<p>I wanted to note that I don't use vector-based software to create my skeletals, I use bitmap-based ones (specifically Photoshop).<br><br>There are many advantages to working with vector-based images, but I find it easier to scale and work in Photoshop (15 years of experience with the software doesn't hurt either).  That said, all skeletals are created with bones on separate layers, and at a resolution that should suffice for any near-term application.<br><br>And yes, reposing in software is immensely easier than redrawing them in analog mediums.</p>
