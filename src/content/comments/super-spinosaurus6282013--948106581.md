<p>For sure Irritator is closer to Spinosaurus than either are to baryonchines, but they still lived on different continents and possibly at different times, so I wouldn't expect them to be clones either. My current reconstruction of the MSMN snout leads to a skull of 162-164 cm in length, but if I enforce a totally Irritator-like back end it's closer to 158 (mostly because of the more vertical quadrate). I should note that these are premax-quadrate measurements, not a maximum length measurement (which would actually be from the snout to the sagittal crest as I've restored it).</p><p>None of this changes my overall scaling of the the length or mass of Spinosaurus.</p>
