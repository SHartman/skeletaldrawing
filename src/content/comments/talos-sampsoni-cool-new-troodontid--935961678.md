<p>Excellent job on the Talos reconstruction.  We do have definite limb proportions for Troodon though, if you count the Two Medicine material as that genus (initially I was hoping that's what Talos was, but then I saw it was from Utah...).  For instance, the adult MOR 748 has a femur 320 mm, tibia 362 mm and metatarsal IV 227 mm, as mentioned by Varricchio et al. (2002) and photographed in Varricchio et al. (2008).</p>
