<p>I think conceptually Smilodon might be an OK comparison, but not because it's grappling in the same way - just that they are both robust and can take and give out a beating.  Smilodon seems to have been extending quadrupedal feline-style grappling in terms of holding on with forelimbs, etc., while Utahraptor presumably was extending bipedal RPR-style grappling.</p>
