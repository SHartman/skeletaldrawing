<p>If the author creates T. rex in a running pose, it would be inherent information that he/she feels that this was plausible. If the Trex is at a fast walk, this would indicate that the author feels that this was the plausible extreme. <br><br>At the risk of lending this one point too much weight - isn't it fantastic to get this added info about the author's views? And if there are various skeletals reflecting various opinions as to what the extreme pose is, then all the better. People will understand that there is discussion, and perhaps some will drift into the mechanics of the discussion itself.</p>
