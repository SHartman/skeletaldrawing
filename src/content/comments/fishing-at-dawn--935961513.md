<p>Thanks David - the water is basically two steps - I got some of the depth of field blur and high frequency noise by creating a custom depth field and a displacement map, applying them in the camera blur and zigzag filters respectively. Then I went in and painted the ripples with the brush tool, varying how hard the edge was and how quickly the paint covered it based on need.</p>
