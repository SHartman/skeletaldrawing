<p>If you're going to do a death pose, it could be exaggerated--neck pulled back and all that, so it really would look like it was on a dissecting table.  The limbs could be in different positions, too.  But it wouldn't have much of a rectangular dimension, and not all the elements would be in the more or less standard "cranial = left, caudal = right" (or vice-versa) orientation, which might screw with some peoples' heads...</p>
