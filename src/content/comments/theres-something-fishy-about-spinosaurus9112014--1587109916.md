<p>Being agnostic is a perfectly reasonable stance, but let me say that while decreasing fermur/tib ratios are normal ontogenetic predictions, shrinking pelvic elements relative to the dorsal column is not known at all. That, along with the fact that all limb and pelvic elements are off by the same amount are why I doubt that there are other explanations than a scaling error at play here. But time (and more data) will tell!</p>
