<p>Matt Wedel has a conference abstract on this (the extended bone pneumacity saves body weight), he estimated that the hollow bones of a Tyrannosaurus would only reduce it's mass by 7%, that's a weight reduction of about 600 kilos compared to a hypothetical 9  tonne rex (for FMNH PR 2081). Spinosaurus probably wouldn't have been heavier because of that, if I had to guess, I'd say MSNM V4047's mass could be 6 - 7.5 tonnes, due to the reasons listed above.</p>
