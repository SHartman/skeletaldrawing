<p>It's highly variable among squamates. Some are almost inline, some have mandibles quite a bit narrower than their upper tooth row. Also, note that crocs (which are basically the only tetrapods in existence with teeth and no lips) do not have an overbite - so why anyone would think that having an overbite is a problem with having lips is beyond me.</p>
