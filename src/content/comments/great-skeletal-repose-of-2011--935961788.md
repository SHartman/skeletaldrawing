<p>Ok, lots of good stuff, I'll try to reply to everyone:<br><br>@Zach - Thanks!  It did occur to me that simply changing 120 or so skeletals would have a large impact on the poses chosen by others, but honestly I feel like the first time around (with GSPs poses) it became a standard as an afterthought, so I thought this would be a great opportunity to get more voices involved, so we get a better result.  You're other thoughts are excellent, and herald some future articles...<br><br>@Jamie - I'm not saying that no one should ever be allowed to use a unique pose.  But there are many cases (including those "march of evolution" diagrams) where it's more useful to have a common pose, so I still think a standard is useful.  I myself repose skeletals to meet the needs of museums, media projects, other researchers, etc.  But I still feel strongly that there should be an open standard for everyone to use.</p>
