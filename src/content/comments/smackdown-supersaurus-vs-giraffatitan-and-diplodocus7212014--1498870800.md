<p>Nice work, Scott -- but where did you get 10 m for <em>Giraffatitan</em>? The widely cited 8.5 m is straight out of Janensch (1950b:102), who measured "Length of the neck along the neural canal" (translated from the German) as 8.78 m. (So, OK, we dropped 28 cm.)</p><p>Oh, and the "<em>Brachiosaurus</em>" neck that Matt illustrated is of course a <em>Giraffatitan</em> neck. It's the Chicago mount, which was filled in with sculptures based on Berlin elements.</p>
