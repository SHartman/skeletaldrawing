<p>Then again, on one hand there are some eels with insane fangs and seem to have no trouble with "lips" The pike eel shows remarkable convergence in snout morphology having both the rosettes and the "notch" between the maxilla and premaxilla. It even shares a similar bone texture with the tip of the snout. In some photos of the living animal there is even what appears to be a pocket for the teeth. On the other hand there are eels with bunches of sicko knives for teeth and no lips.</p>
