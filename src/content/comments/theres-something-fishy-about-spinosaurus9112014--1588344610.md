<p>The photos of the neotype specimen do seem to show a transition in centrum length that increases posteriorly. What is also apparent is the fact that the pelvis is much lighter in photographic tone and seems to have been put together from numerous fragments. A great deal of it appears to have been reconstructed in plaster. I wonder if it is all present and correctly reconstructed?<br>The Ilium, especially so.</p>
