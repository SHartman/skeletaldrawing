<p>Unfortunately, no nasal is known in "true" megalosaurids, so we have to wait for actual specimens.<br>I do not see nasal and lacrimal ornamentations as strictly co-variant. For example, Guanlong nasal crest is way more elaborated than Allosaurus nasal ornamentation, even if its lacrimal is way less ornamented than in the latter.</p>
