<p>From what I know, Stromer was only in Egypt with Markgraf from late 1910-early 1911 and Markgraf was on his own from 1911 until his death in 1916. Stromer's role in discovering and excavating the Bahariya material has been vastly overstated by many sources, when in reality it was Markgraf that did most of the work.</p><p>The situations with Stromer and Markgraf and the unnamed collector and Ibrahim et al. are really not that different. In both cases we have commercial collectors who are familiar with the area working closely with museum professionals to locate and excavate material.</p>
