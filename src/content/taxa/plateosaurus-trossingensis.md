_Plateosaurus_ is a fairly large (6 m / 20 foot long) bipedal sauropodomorph from the Late Triassic of Europe. It is known from numerous skeletons, many of them remarkably complete, documenting the classic "prosauropod-grade" features of a long-necked, big-stomached, two-legged bipedal plant-eater. 

The specimen these reconstructions are based on (GPIT/RE/7288) has the honor of being one of the first complete dinosaur skeletons to be scanned digitally and then exhaustively compared to various museum mounts and illustrated skeletal reconstructions (in [Mallison, 2010](https://bioone.org/journals/acta-palaeontologica-polonica/volume-55/issue-3/app.2009.0075/The-Digital-Plateosaurus-II--An-Assessment-of-the-Range/10.4202/app.2009.0075.full)). Happily, these images compared favorably to the 3d version.
