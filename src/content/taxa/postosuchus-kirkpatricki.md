_Postosuchus_ was a Late Triassic rauisuchid that lived in southern North America. Rauisuchids were a group of crocodile-line archosaurs, not dinosaurs, although their apparently bipedal (two-legged) locomotion made _Postosuchus_ superficially resemble theropod dinosaurs. Living at a time when dinosaurs were still ascending, it would have been an apex predator, with a deep skull and powerful bite.
