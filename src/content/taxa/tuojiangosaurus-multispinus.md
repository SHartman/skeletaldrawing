_Tuojiangosaurus_ was a stegosaur, a plated dinosaur from the Late Jurassic of China, about 160 million years ago. One of the best-known Asian stegosaurs, _Tuojiangosaurus_ had a double row of tall pointed plates along its back and two pairs of spikes on its tail.
