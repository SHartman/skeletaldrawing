_Psitttacosaurus_ was an early ceratopsian from the Early Cretaceous of China, roughly 125–110 million years ago. About a meter long, _Psittacosaurus_ was a small bipedal plant-eater named for its deep, parrot-like beak, and predates the large frills and horns of later ceratopsians.
