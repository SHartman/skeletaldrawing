_Suskityrannus_ was a small tyrannosauroid from the Late Cretaceous of New Mexico. Growing to only about 1.3 meters (4 feet) long, _Suskityrannus_ documents an early stage in tyrannosaur evolution, before the lineage produced the giant apex predators of the latest Cretaceous. Like other small tyrannosauroids, _Suskityrannus_ was almost certainly covered in bristly, fur-like feathers.
