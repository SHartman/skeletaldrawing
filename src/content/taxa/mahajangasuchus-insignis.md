A robust, semiaquatic notosuchian from the Late Cretaceous of Madagascar. _Mahajangasuchus_ combined a broad, deep skull and stout conical teeth with a heavy shield of interlocking dorsal armor running down its back. It was probably less terrestrial than other notosuchians, although at over 4 meters (13 feet) you wouldn't want to go wading near one if they had survived to the modern day.
