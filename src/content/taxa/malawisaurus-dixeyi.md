_Malawisaurus_ was a Cretaceous titanosaur from (you guessed it) Malawi. At around 10 m (32 feet) long, it wasn't a small animal, but was smaller than the average titanosaur. The type specimen (SAM-7405) presereved some of the dermal armor (osteoderms) that ran along its back and tail. While the exact placement of the armor is still debated, the arrangement proposed by Vidal, et al. is followed here in the armored version of the skeletal.
