A tyrannosaurid from the Late Cretaceous of Utah, _Teratophoneus_ grew to around 5 meters (17 feet) long. _Teratophoneus_ had a relatively short, deep skull and was a mid-sized predator of the ancient landmass of Laramidia, in western North America.
