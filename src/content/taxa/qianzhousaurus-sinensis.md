_Qianzhousaurus_ was a tyrannosaurid from the Late Cretaceous of southern China, nicknamed "Pinocchio rex" for its exceptionally long, slender snout. It belongs to a distinctive group of equally long-snouted Asian tyrannosaurs known as alioramids, that were also longer-legged and more lightly built than giants like _Tarbosaurus_ and _Tyrannosaurus._
