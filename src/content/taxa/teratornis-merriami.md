A large extinct bird of the Pleistocene, related to living condors and among the many species preserved in the La Brea tar pits of California. With a wingspan of roughly 3.5 to 4 metres, _Teratornis_ was a soaring predator and scavenger that died out around the end of the last Ice Age.
