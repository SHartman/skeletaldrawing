A Late Triassic phytosaur — a long-snouted, semiaquatic archosauriform on the crocodile line of archosaurs, not a dinosaur. _Paleorhinus_ shows the phytosaur hallmark of nostrils drawn back atop the skull near the eyes, rather than at the snout tip.
