_Khaan mckennai_ was an oviraptorosaur theropod from the Late Cretaceous of Mongolia. Like other oviraptors, _Khaan_ would have been winged, and probably laid eggs in ground nests that one or more parents sat in and protected. This skeletal is based on IGM 100/1002, a particularly complete subadult individual that was missing only the middle and back of the tail.
