A small thescelosaurid ornithopod from the Late Cretaceous of Montana. _Orodromeus_ was a swift, bipedal plant-eater known from the same region as the nesting dinosaur _Maiasaura_. There is a reasonable chance that _Orodromeus_ (and many other small ornithopods) burrowed, as has been documented in its close relative, _Oryctodromeus_.
