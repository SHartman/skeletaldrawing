A megalosauroid theropod from the Late Jurassic Morrison Formation of the western United States. At 5 m (16 feet) _Marshosaurus_ was a relatively modest-sized predator, though it shared its Morrison habitat with far larger carnivores such as _Allosaurus_ and _Torvosaurus._
