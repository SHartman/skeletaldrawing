_Ouranosaurus_ was a large iguanodont from the Early Cretaceous of Niger. _Ouranosaurus_ bore tall neural spines forming a ridge or sail along its back, and had a broad, flattened snout. Although it could likely walk either quadrupedally or bipedally, a recent study confirmed it appears to be most adapted for getting around on four-legs.
