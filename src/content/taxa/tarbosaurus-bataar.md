_Tarbosaurus_ was a large tyrannosaurid from the Late Cretaceous of Mongolia, and a close relative of its more famous cousin, _Tyrannosaurus._ Reaching 10 m (33 feet) in length, it was almost as large as _T. rex_, making _Tarbosaurus_ the apex predator of the Asian floodplains where it lived.
