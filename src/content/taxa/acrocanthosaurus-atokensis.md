_Acrocanthosaurus_ was a large predatory dinosaur that lived during the Early Cretaceous in North America, roughly 113 million years ago. A relative of _Gigantosaurus_ and _Carcharodontosaurus_, and somewhat more distantly to _Allosaurus_, it grew to 12 (40 feet) long. _Acrocanthosaurus_ was notable for the tall neural spines running down its back, which supported either a low ridge or a muscular hump.
