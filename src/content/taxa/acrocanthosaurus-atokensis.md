A large predatory dinosaur from the Early Cretaceous of North America, roughly 113 million years ago. Reaching around 12 metres in length, _Acrocanthosaurus_ was a carcharodontosaurid notable for the tall neural spines running down its back, which supported a low ridge or muscular hump.
