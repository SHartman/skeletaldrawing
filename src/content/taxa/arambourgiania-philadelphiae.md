A Late Cretaceous azhdarchid pterosaur — a flying reptile, not a dinosaur — from the phosphate beds of Jordan, and among the largest ever to fly, with a wingspan around ten metres. _Arambourgiania_ is known chiefly from an enormously elongated neck vertebra some 78 centimetres long.
