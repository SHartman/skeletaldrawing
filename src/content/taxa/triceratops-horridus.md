_Triceratops_ is the quintessential large horned dinosaur from the latest Cretaceous of western North America, living about 68–66 million years ago. _Triceratops horridus_ carried two long brow horns, a shorter nasal horn, and a solid bony frill. It is thought to have been a bit older than _Triceratops prorsus_, which may have been its direct descendant.
