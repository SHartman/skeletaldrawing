An Early Jurassic ichthyosaur from the marine shales of Dorset and Somerset, England, and the type genus of the whole ichthyosaur order. _Ichthyosaurus_ had a dolphin-like body, four crescent-shaped paddles, and large eyes ringed by bony sclerotic plates.
