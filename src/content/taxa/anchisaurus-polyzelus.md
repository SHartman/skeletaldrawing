A surprisingly lightly built sauropodomorph from the Early Jurassic of eastern North America, _Anchisaurus_ was one of the first dinosaurs to be scientifically described from the United States. Although traditionally thought to be quite a basal member of the early, two-legged sauropod relatives, more recent studies suggest it was quite a bit closer to the origin of sauropods, despite being modest in size and lightly built.
