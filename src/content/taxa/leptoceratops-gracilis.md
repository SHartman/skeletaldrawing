A small leptoceratopsid, one of the primitive horned dinosaurs, from the latest Cretaceous of Alberta and Montana. _Leptoceratops_ lacked the large frill and horns of its ceratopsid relatives. With a big head, short tail and moderate size these dinosaurs are sometimes likened to wild boars. _Leptoceratops_ has been found in a mass-death assemblage, suggesting it lived in groups.
