_Thescelosaurus_ was a small ornithischian plant-eater from the very end of the Cretaceous in western North America, about 66 million years ago. _Thescelosaurus_ was a sturdily built bipedal runner that persisted right up to the extinction event, making it one of the last known non-avian dinosaurs.
