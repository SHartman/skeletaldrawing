_Tapejara_ is the eponymous tapejarid pterosaur, a group of crested, flying reptiles that are closely related to dinosaurs without actually being one. Living in the Early Cretaceou, and found in the Santana Formation of Brazil, _Tapejara_ was a small (1.3 m / 4.5 foot wingspan) toothless flier. Skin impresions show the bony cfrest was enhanced dramatically by a soft-tissue extension that was larger than the rest of the head.
