_Melanorosaurus_ was a good-sized (8 m / 25 foot long), quadrupedal sauropodomorph from the Late Triassic of South Africa. Originally more material was placed into _Melanorosaurus_ until high resolution stratigraphy showed many of the referred specimens were from the Early Jurassic. This style of short-limbed quadrupedalism seems to be typical of the shift from bipedal herbivores towards the more classic body plan of true sauropods.
