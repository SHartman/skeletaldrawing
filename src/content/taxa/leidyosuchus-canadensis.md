A crocodylian from the Late Cretaceous of North America, long thought to be an early alligatoroid. Exactly where _Leidyosuchus_ falls is no uncertain, as the same recent work that unsettled the phylogenetic position of _Deinosuchus_ also pulled _Leidysuchus_ out of Alligatoroidea toward the crocodylian stem.
