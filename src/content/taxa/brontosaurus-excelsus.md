_Brontosaurus_ is probably the most famous of the Late Jurassic Morrison Formation sauropods. A diplodocid, it's heavily built like _Apatosaurus_ rather than _Diplodocus_. The name _Brontosaurus_ was made famous in the U.S. due to an outreach campaign by Sinclair Oil that lasted from the 1930s to the 1960s. Ironically, during most of the 20th century the name _Brontosaurus_ was considered a junior name of _Apatosaurus_; it was only revived as a distinct genus in 2015.
