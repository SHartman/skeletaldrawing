A giant diplodocid of the Morrison Formation — among the longest dinosaurs known, though the length estimates rest on incomplete material. Two specimens are documented here: the holotype NMMNH 3690, first described as *Seismosaurus*, and AMNH 223, long referred to *Diplodocus longus* before that name was set aside. Both are known from partial skeletons, so each is shown as known material rather than a full reconstruction.
