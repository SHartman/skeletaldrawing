The classic woolly mammoth, a cold-adapted elephant of the Pleistocene mammoth steppe. _Mammuthus primigenius_ had a domed head, small ears, a dense coat of fur, and long, strongly curved tusks; isolated island populations survived into the mid-Holocene.
