_Daspletosaurus torosus_ is a tyrannosaurid theropod from the Late Cretaceous of Alberta, Canada. Both phylogenetically and in terms of being a large, robustly built apex predator it's very close to _Tyrannosaurus_. It lived alongside smaller, more lightly built albertosaurine tyrannosaurs, who presumably fed on smaller or faster prey, while the deep skull and powerful jaws allowed the more robust _Daspletosaurus_ to feed it on larger animals in their environment (and no doubt to bully the occasional albertosaurine off of a kill).
