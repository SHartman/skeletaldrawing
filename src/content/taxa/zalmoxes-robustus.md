A small ornithopod from the Late Cretaceous of Romania, about 70–66 million years ago. _Zalmoxes_ lived on the ancient Hațeg Island of the European archipelago and, like other island-dwelling dinosaurs there, was comparatively small, a possible case of insular dwarfism.
