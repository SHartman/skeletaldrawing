An early tyrannosauroid from the Late Cretaceous of Alabama, about 77 million years ago. _Appalachiosaurus_ lived in Appalachia, the eastern landmass separated from western North America by an inland sea during most of the Late Cretaceous. While the remains are somewhat scrappy as compared to many tyrannosauroids from western North America and eastern Asia, _Appalachiosaurus_ is one of the most complete predatory dinosaurs known from that region.
