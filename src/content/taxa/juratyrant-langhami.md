A small tyrannosauroid from the Late Jurassic of England. The species was first described as *Stokesosaurus langhami* (Benson, 2008), then moved to its own genus, *Juratyrant*, by Brusatte & Benson in 2013. This skeletal was originally drawn when the material was referred to *Stokesosaurus*, and is being revised to reflect the split — the American *Stokesosaurus clevelandi* itself remains too fragmentary to restore.
