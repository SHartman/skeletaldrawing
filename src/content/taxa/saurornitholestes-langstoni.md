_Saurornitholestes_ was a dromaeosaurid (small-to-medium, sickle-clawed predatory theropods) from the Late Cretaceous of western North America. _Saurornitholestes_ was a lightly built, agile hunter. It lived alongside _Bambiraptor_, who is similar enough that some have suggested the two might be the same species, although that is not currently the consensus view.
