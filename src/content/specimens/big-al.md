MOR 693, also known as "Big Al," is a strikingly complete subadult _Allosaurus jimmadseni_ from the Morrison Formation of Wyoming. The specimen is famous both for how much of the skeleton was recovered and for the many healed injuries and pathologies its bones record. It doesn't hurt that the BBC also made it the star of a Walking With Dinosaurs documentary (The Ballad of Big Al) in 2000. In a twist of irony, despite the nickname Big Al was not yet fully grown.
