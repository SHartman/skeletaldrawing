MOR 693, "Big Al," is a strikingly complete subadult *Allosaurus jimmadseni* from the Morrison Formation of Wyoming — famous both for how much of the skeleton was recovered and for the many healed injuries and pathologies its bones record. Despite the nickname, it was not yet full-grown.
