It’s been a long time coming, but on behalf of my coauthors it is my pleasure to introduce the world to a new dinosaur, *Hesperornithoides miessleri*. *Hesperornithoides* is a small, winged theropod from the Late Jurassic of Wyoming that appears to be most closely related to [*Troodon* and kin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Troodontidae).

You can find the paper [here](https://peerj.com/articles/7247/).

I’ve written up the basics and answered the most common questions about *Hesperornithoides* [in this blog post.](http://www.skeletaldrawing.com/home/hesperornithoides-faq7102019)
